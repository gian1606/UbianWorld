VISA RELATED CONCERNS

International students may be allowed to enroll if they have the following types of visa:

  1. 9 (f) – Student visa
  2. 9 (e), 9 (e-1) or 9 (e-2) – Foreign government official or dependent
  3. 47 (a) (2) – Exchange fellow or scholar sponsored by an international organization
  4. 9 (g) – Pre-arranged employment (Working visa/Missionary)
  5. PD 218 – Foreign investor
  6. 9 (d) – Treaty trader
  7. 13, 13 (a) to 13 (g) – Permanent resident
  8. SRRV – Special Resident Retiree Visa
  9. SIRV – Special Investor Resident Visa

The issue of Student Visa is valid for one year and succeeding extensions cover the academic year. However, students are given an extension period that is shorter than 6 months when nearing the completion of its program.
